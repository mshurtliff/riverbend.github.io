Stock Plan Pricing


 

INOUYE DESIGN STOCK HOME PLAN PRICING

  • Pricing for construction documents (plans) is based on square footage of above grade living space, including bonus rooms.
  • Prices do not include any required engineering fees, including but not limited to structural, civil, geotechnical, mechanical, and electrical.
  • Auxiliary buildings such as detached garages, pool houses, gazebos, etc. are subject to additional charges.
  • Pricing is for a standard “Builder’s Set” of plans.
  • Any additional changes or requirements made by city/ county officials or owners will be charged the standard hourly rate.
  • All payments are non-refundable and final payment must be received before plans can be released from our office.
  • Prices include a standard site plan and (4) sets of plans and a set of pdf files.
