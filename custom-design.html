Design Process

We can start with:


  • 1. A plan you like that needs changes


  • 2. Multiple plans that you like parts of


  • 3. A list of requirements



Step Two:

Next we meet together either in person or virtually via zoom to discuss desires, needs, and priorities



Step Three:

Then I design your custom home. Multiple revisions help to ensure you get exactly what you want.

 



Step Four:

Finally, you recieve custom plans to send out for bidding.

 


Step Five:

Revisions after bidding happen and are included in the plan's price.

PRICING BREAKDOWN


The price is based on the final square footage

 
  • $0.40 per main floor square footage

  • $0.20 per basement square footage

  • $0.10 per garage square footage

  • A $500.00 deposit is required prior to starting a project

  • The remaining balance is due before the final plans are delivered

  • All necessary travel expenses may be additional
