About Us

River Bend Drafting and Design specializes in personalized custom homes that meet your needs, budget, and timeframe. River Bend Drafting and Design was formed in 2017 by Elise Shurtliff to provide innovative design to create extraordinary hosing projects that reflect the needs, desires, and personality of each home owner. Integral to providing quality service is maintaining attention to detail, utilizing 3D computer technology, incorporating specific requirements from the client, and treating each client as a person and not a number.
River Bend Drafting and Design believes the perfect home comes in many sizes and shapes; it can be a brand new design or an old house remodel. what matters is your house fits you, and we want to help you get there.

 

River Bend Drafting and Design utilizes the latest technologies in computer aided design/drafting.


About Us

River Bend Drafting and Design uses Chief Architect modeling software, which allows a project to be built in a virtual environment where the design process can be taken to a higher level of creativity. This helps the designer and clients to visualize the design in a more realistic perspective. Money and time can be saved due to a decrease in change orders since the homeowner is able to better visualize the final project and make adjustments during the design phase.
